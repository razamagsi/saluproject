Prof. Dr. Agha Nadia Pathan

Incharge

INTRODUCTION


The Institute of Gender Studies was established in 2013. The main objectives of the institute are to train the students and workers of this region in Gender Studies, to promote co-operation in inter-disciplinary relationship with others teaching/research departments and NGO establishment, and to arrange seminars and workshops in order to raise the awareness about gender and its implications. The institute offers multidisciplinary courses that explore the making and meaning of gender – femininity and masculinity across cultures and social formations.

VISION:


Becoming a world-class institute in the field of gender studies and promoting the subject as a learning discipline in the remote areas.

Mission


To enhance understanding of gender and how it is socially constructed
To create an awareness about gender in the context of South Asia in general and Pakistan in particular
To analyze critically the status of women in Pakistani society
To evaluate development approaches worldwide and their effects on women
To explore political and theoretical dimensions of feminist pedagogy

PROGRAMS OFFERD


  • BS (Four -Year)
  • M.A Gender Studies (Two-Year)
  • Diploma in Computer Skills (One-Year)
